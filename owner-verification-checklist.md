# Afreen Unisex Salon Owner-Verification Checklist

Before publishing, confirm or replace:

- Exact public business address, removing any promotional wording if it is not part of the address.
- Opening hours for weekdays, Saturday, Sunday, and holidays.
- Current service list, service descriptions, prices if the owner wants to show them, and appointment rules.
- Real salon photos with permission to use them on the website.
- Individual review excerpts only if they are accurate, current, and permitted for use.
- Google Business Profile/map link and geo coordinates.
- Preferred website domain, official email if any, and any social profile links.
- Any required disclaimers for beauty, skin, chemical, bridal, or appointment services.
